Serving UT and the Austin community since 1956...

Service is one of the four pillars of our organization. Texas Spirits accounts for over 2,000 hours of service each semester! Spirits complete their service hours both inside and outside the organization. We volunteer in a variety of events and projects each semester, often collaborating with other student organizations to work for great causes.

Texas Spirits presents Band Jam!

Our largest annual event is Band Jam, a huge charity concert benefiting the Make-A-Wish Foundation, a nonprofit organization dedicated to granting the wishes of hospitalized and terminally ill children. Band Jam started in 1995 and has been getting bigger and better every year.

Check out the Band Jam website to donate and for more info
