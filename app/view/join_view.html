What does it take to be a Texas Spirit

We seek ladies who possess the characteristics of leadership, scholarship, potential, and all-around enthusiasm for what it means to be a Longhorn. We are looking for individuals who are spirited and have a sincere desire to serve the University of Texas and the Austin community. Finally, in order to experience and enjoy the benefits of our organization to the fullest, a substantial time commitment is encouraged. Please consider this when applying to be a Texas Spirit.

Requirements:

In order to become a Texas Spirit Weenie, you must be a registered freshman or sophomore with a GPA of 2.5 or above.

Applicants who are planning to study abroad or to not be enrolled in-residence for any other reason during Spring 2013 or Fall 2013 are ineligible to apply.
