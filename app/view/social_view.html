Girls Do Just Wanna Have Fun!

Texas Spirits know how to have fun! Whether among us, or with various groups at mixers and tailgates, the variety of activities we participate in allows everyone to have a great time with their Spirit Sisters.

Mixers

Our mixers give us a chance to mingle with some of the other male groups on campus. These weekly socials are usually themed and are always a blast!

Formal

This happens at the end of the fall semester. Girls usually bring dates and have dinner before the event. This past fall, we danced the night away at Qua in Downtown Austin!

Casual

Texas Spirits invites their alumni back for a casual every spring. It is a great opportunity to talk with the alumni and see how many traditions have remained the same, and also new traditions that have been implemented along the way.

Destination Unknown

Our Special Projects Haunts plan a fun night at a secret location. We get on a bus with our dates, only knowing what our attire should be. It's a fun surprise when we arrive at our destination unknown! Six Flags Fiesta Texas, San Antonio Riverwalk, and Schlitterbahn Water Park in New Braunfels have all been past DU locations!

That's not all

Weenie Active Mixer, A-Weenie Oahu, Slip-N-Slide, Crush, Date Dash, Active Boat Party, Dad's Day Baseball Game, Mom's Day Brunch, and Initiation Banquet.
